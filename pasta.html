Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Pasta of Choice (Regular or GF)
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 5â€“6 Cloves Minced Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Each: Minced Parsley, Thyme, Rosemary
  • 2 1/2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1 1/2 Cups Shredded Cheese (Mozzarella and/or White Cheddar)
  • 1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • Optional: Red Pepper Flake

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta to preferred doneness and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, add your butter. Toss in your garlic and cook until fragrant and the butter slightly browns.
  3. Mix, and add your minced herbs. Stir for about 1 minute. Reduce heat to low and pour in your heavy cream. When the cream is warm but not too hot, add your cheese and stir. Let this simmer on low until the sauce is combined. This will get nice and thick.
  4. Toss in your cooked pasta and sprinkle with parmesan cheese! Serve immediately and enjoy!
Tip: For extra spice, add red pepper flakes while cooking the garlic.
